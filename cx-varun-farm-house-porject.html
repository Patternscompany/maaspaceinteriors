CX Varun Farm House
Construction Project

MA Space Interiors proudly led the construction and interior execution of the CX Varun Farm House — an exclusive residential retreat that blends luxury living with natural surroundings. From conceptual architectural designs to the final interior detailing, our team delivered a serene and elegant farmhouse tailored for comfort, relaxation, and timeless appeal.

The project showcases spacious layouts, premium finishes, and bespoke interiors that seamlessly integrate indoor and outdoor spaces, creating a perfect sanctuary for leisure and gatherings with family and friends.

The farmhouse was meticulously crafted to reflect a harmonious balance of modern elegance and rustic charm, ensuring both aesthetic beauty and long-lasting functionality.

  • Complete civil construction and architectural development
  • Luxurious interior design for living spaces, bedrooms & recreational zones
  • Custom-crafted furniture, lighting, and decor accents
  • Premium flooring, wooden finishes, and elegant ceilings
  • Thoughtful space planning for comfort, privacy, and seamless indoor-outdoor flow
